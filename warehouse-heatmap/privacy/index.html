Unclocked Warehouse 3D Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Introduction

Unclocked Warehouse 3D is a Microsoft Power BI custom visual from Unclocked Studios LLC for warehouse layout and operational analysis. This Privacy Policy explains how customer data is handled.

Data Collection

Unclocked Studios LLC does not collect customer data through Unclocked Warehouse 3D.

Data Sharing and Sale

Unclocked Studios LLC does not share customer data and does not sell customer data.

Customer Data

Any warehouse, coordinate, layout, operational, or business data used with the visual remains under the customer's control in their own Power BI environment. Unclocked Studios LLC does not receive, store, transmit, share, or sell that data.

Power BI Environment

The visual runs inside Microsoft Power BI and uses the fields, measures, filters, and formatting settings provided by the report author. Data handling by Microsoft Power BI is governed by the customer's Microsoft environment and applicable Microsoft terms.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@unclocked.app.
